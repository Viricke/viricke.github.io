Diamond Kidd

Diamond was the first sim who wanted to achieve something in the world.
She has a long history, she is the oldest living sim troughout Sim Land.
Her ambitions led her to be a 5 star celebrity and global superstar in the sim world.
After she was a 5 star celebrity she had a 'Placement Ceremony', which was about placing
a celebrity tile on Starlight Boulevard. It was a rainy day.

She starred in the 'The Sheriff of Alpha Centaury in 0003.

Diamond and Jade Rosa share the same father to their children.
